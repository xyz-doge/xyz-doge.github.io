About Me

My name is Xiang Yingzhe. I am an IBI1 student at the Zhejiang University - University of Edinburgh (ZJE) Institute.

I have a wide range of hobbies, such as playing the piano and using music to relieve stress daily. I also enjoy football and, as a member of the ZJE football team, I participated in the Trailblazer Cup football match. Through physical training and teamwork, I have learned perseverance and tolerance.

Additionally, I was the fourth debater in the 8th campus White Horse Cup debate competition, which broadened my speculative thinking while analyzing debate topics. I was also honored as the best debater of the year.

In terms of work, I have actively engaged in the affairs of the Quality Development Center of the Communist Party of China and served as the League branch secretary of my class since college.
